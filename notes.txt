========== 2012-06-12 ==========

A Portfolio Tracker.

Technologies: EmberJS, Bootstrap, Rails.

Allow users to search financial assets (by company name, ticker symbol, etc.), and add them to their
portfolio. Allow them to set the date they bought them at (and transaction cost, and auto-populate
closing day price, but allow override). Take in to account dividends paid.
